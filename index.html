Whom is this course for?

Freshworks STS Software Academy is designed for students aged 18 to 21 who are unable to continue their education beyond the 12th/diploma. Students from Tamil medium government schools, those from low-income households, single-parent families from rural areas. Students who are hungry for opportunities to improve their lives and are ready to learn any skill required, with effort and the right attitude.
